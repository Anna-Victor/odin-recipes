Fruit Salad Recipe

Description

Fruit salads are one of the easiest foods to prepare. They mainly contain heathly fruits served in a liquid such as cream, mayonaise, peanut butter or wine. They are also very healthy and can be served as appetizers. Fruit salads are sometimes called fruit cocktails or fruit cups when served in a small container.They are many fruit salads, ranging from the basic (no nuts, marshmallows, or dressing) to the moderately sweet and then to the sweet. There are a number of recipes for fruit salas that contain different kinds of fruit, or that use a different kind of sauce other than the fruit's own juice or syrup. Common ingredients used in fruit salads include strawberries, pineapples, honeydew, watermelon, grapes, and kiwifruts. Various recipes may call for the addition of nuts, fruits, juices, certain vegetables, yoghurt, or other ingredients.

In this recipe, we shall use the following fruits to prepare:

  • pineapples
  • mandarin oranges
  • cherries
  • marshmallows
Additionally, we shall add sour cream to the fruits to give them a sweet sour taste. The dish can be served as an appetizer or after the main course.

Ingredients

For this recipe you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1(20 ounce) can pineapple chunks,drained
  • 2(11 ounce) cans mandarin oranges,drained
  • 1(10 ounce) jar maraschino cherries,halved
  • 1(16 ounce) package miniature marshmallows
  • 1(16 ounce) container sour cream

Steps

The directions to be followed during preparartion are as follows:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the pineapples, oranges, cherries, and marshmallows.
  2. Add the sour cream to be bowl with the fruits and combine.
  3. Chill and serve.
